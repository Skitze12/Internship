About us

At Sporty Fitness Club (SFC), our aim is to inspire and empower individuals to achieve their fitness goals while fostering a supportive community dedicated to health and well being. We focus on a holistic approach to fitness, encouraging not only physical strength but also mental relief and nutritional balance. Our goal is to let our members to achieve a better lifestyle.

How We Started..

Established in 2005, SFC was founded by proficient fitness OG, named "Ronnie Coleman", who was the Greatest Bodybuilder of his prime. He aimed to create a space where people could transform their lives through fitness and wellness. Over the years we have grown into a hub of individuals seeking a holistic approach to health.

Facilities and Services

SFC boasts state-of-the-art equipment,dedicated workout areas for cardio, strength, training and group exercise classes including Yoga, Zumba, and high-intensity interval training (HIIT). Our certified personal trainers offer tailored programs and nutritional guidance to help you reach your fitness milestones. At SFC, we believe in giving back. We participate alot in local charity programs. Organize fitness events for community outreach and exposure. Also, we sponsor health workshops to promote wellness beyond our gym walls.

SFC Trainers

Our team includes Certified trainers with great experstise in various fitness disciplines. They're very dedicated for guiding and motivating our members on their fitness journey, ensuring that each individual member, who is sticking with us, recieves personalized attention and support, at Gym and online also.
